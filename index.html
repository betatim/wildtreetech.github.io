Wild Tree Tech - the data science experts.

Wild Tree Tech is a small company focused on education and independent data-science software. Wild Tree Tech offers intermediate to advanced level training courses to individuals and corporate clients.

Tim Head

Science, machine-learning, and triathlon.

Tim is a consultant, developer and educator.

He develops open source software that is used by thousands of businesses and organizations to effectively model and learn about their data. He created scikit-optimize, a blackbox optimisation toolkit and maintains mybinder.org, a data-science platform. Before founding Wild Tree Tech he was a scientist at CERN leading small teams to innovate on pattern reconstruction, allowing CERN to process data at an unprecedented rate.

In his free time Tim trains for triathlons.
