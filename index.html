Tim Head

Science, statistics, and triathlon.

Tim is a scientist and tool maker at heart. He believes that better information leads to smarter decisions.

He develops open source software that is used by thousands of businesses and organizations to effectively model and learn about their data. He writes about machine-learning techniques. He works with organisations like the ICRC to improve humanitarian disaster handling.
