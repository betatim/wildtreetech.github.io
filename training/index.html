Training courses

These courses will bring your staff up to speed with modern data analysis techniques.

Passing on our knowledge

Wild Tree Technologies offers training sessions on the fundamental skills needed for data driven decision making and artificial intelligence.

The training courses are highly interactive, tailored to the participants and strictly limited to ten people per course.

  • Deep learning from scratch, an intense course that will have you implement a deep neural network from scratch and finish with state of the art tools. Designed for working data scientists who want to take their understanding of every day tools to the next level.
  • Modern AI techniques, a course that will get you up to speed with modern AI techniques. Designed for professional software developers, scientists, and engineers. Example syllabus and material.

Courses are designed with scientists, engineers, entrepreneurs, financial analysts, and data scientists in mind. Content is fine tuned for each audience. If you would like to learn how to use modern software tools in your specific work environment, you are our target audience! Our targeted courses address both scientific and programming best practices.

Courses are available in both English and German.
