Passing on our knowledge

Wild Tree Technologies develops and teaches workshops on the fundamental skills needed for data driven decision making.

Our training courses are highly interactive, follow modern teaching practices, are tailored to the participants, are taught on-site and are strictly limited to six people per course.

We believe in open-source tools and have a bias towards python. We like R as well, and even c++.

Courses are designed with scientists, engineers, social entrepreneurs, financial analysts, and data scientists in mind. Content is fine tuned for each audience. If you would like to learn how to use modern software tools in your specific work environment, you are our target audience! Our targeted courses address both scientific and programming best practices.

Courses are available in both English and German.
