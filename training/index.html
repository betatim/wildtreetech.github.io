Deep learning from scratch

Modern machine-learning for businesses

A five day course that will get you up to speed with modern techniques and libraries. We will cover general techniques, and use pandas, scikit-learn, TensorFlow, and keras to build solutions for real world business problems. Designed for professional software developers, data-scientists, scientists, and engineers.

We believe that the best way to learn is by doing. Come prepared to write code. We will alternate between a short lecture, writing code, and short demos over the course of each day. After each iteration sample solutions are distributed so that at the end of the day you will have a working project.

Courses are designed with developers, scientists, engineers, entrepreneurs, financial analysts, and data scientists in mind. Content is fine tuned for each audience. If you would like to learn how to use modern software tools in your specific work environment, you are the target audience!

Instructors speak fluent English and German.
